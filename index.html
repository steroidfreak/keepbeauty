Introduction to Bojin Therapy

Bojin is a traditional body therapy originating from China. It primarily involves manipulating muscles and fascia using specialized techniques to relax the body, relieve muscle tension, and promote blood circulation. This therapy focuses on balancing the entire body and is suitable for people of all ages, especially those who sit for long periods, suffer from sports injuries, or experience excessive stress.

  • Relieves Muscle Tension: Bojin effectively relaxes tight muscles, reducing pain and discomfort.
  • Improves Blood Circulation: By stimulating blood flow, it promotes metabolism, helping the body regain vitality.
  • Enhances Flexibility: It improves muscle and joint flexibility, making movements smoother.
  • Reduces Stress: Bojin helps relax both body and mind, alleviating stress and anxiety, and boosting overall well-being.
  • Aids in Recovery: It is ideal for post-workout recovery and injury rehabilitation, helping the body heal faster.

1. Does Bojin hurt?

You may feel some pressure during the Bojin process, but it is generally not intensely painful. The therapist will adjust the pressure based on your physical condition and tolerance.

2. How many treatments are needed to see results?

Each person's body is different. It is usually recommended to undergo at least 3-5 sessions to notice significant results. Regular Bojin treatments over time can provide more lasting benefits.

3. Is Bojin suitable for everyone?

Bojin is generally suitable for most people. However, if you have serious health issues (such as fractures, tumors, etc.), it is advisable to consult a doctor first. Pregnant women or those with certain conditions should seek professional advice before undergoing treatment.

4. What should I be mindful of after the treatment?

It is recommended to drink plenty of water to help detoxify the body after treatment. Avoid strenuous exercise and heavy physical labor to allow your body adequate time to recover.

Initial Consultation: During your first appointment, the therapist will conduct a detailed discussion with you to understand your health condition, areas of discomfort, and desired results.

Assessment and Preparation: The therapist will assess your body condition and adjust the treatment plan accordingly. You may choose to wear comfortable clothing for ease during the session.

Bojin Therapy: The therapist will use professional techniques to target specific muscles and fascia, adjusting the pressure in real-time based on your responses throughout the session.

Post-Treatment Evaluation: After the session, the therapist will discuss the results with you, assess the outcome, and provide follow-up recommendations.

Follow-up Care: Based on the treatment effects, a subsequent treatment plan will be suggested to ensure optimal results.

拨筋疗法介绍

拨筋是源自中国的传统身体疗法，主要通过专业的技巧操作肌肉和筋膜，放松身体，缓解肌肉紧张，促进血液循环。此疗法注重全身平衡，适合所有年龄段的人群，尤其是久坐、运动受伤或压力过大的人群。

  • 缓解肌肉紧张：拨筋有效放松紧绷的肌肉，减轻疼痛和不适。
  • 改善血液循环：通过刺激血液流动，促进新陈代谢，帮助身体恢复活力。
  • 增强柔韧性：它改善肌肉和关节的柔韧性，使运动更加流畅。
  • 减轻压力：拨筋有助于放松身心，减轻压力和焦虑，提升整体健康。
  • 促进康复：适合运动后恢复和受伤康复，帮助身体更快恢复。

1. 拨筋会疼吗？

在拨筋过程中您可能会感到一些压力，但通常不会感到剧烈疼痛。技师会根据您的身体状况和耐受度调整压力。

2. 需要多少次疗程才能看到效果？

每个人的身体状况不同。通常建议进行至少 3-5 次疗程才能看到显著效果。定期的拨筋疗程能带来更持久的益处。

3. 拨筋适合所有人吗？

拨筋通常适合大多数人。但如果您有严重的健康问题（如骨折、肿瘤等），建议先咨询医生。孕妇或有特殊情况者，应先寻求专业建议。

4. 治疗后需要注意什么？

治疗后建议多喝水以帮助排毒。避免剧烈运动和重体力劳动，让身体有足够时间恢复。

初次咨询：在您的第一次预约中，技师将与您详细讨论您的健康状况、不适部位和期望结果。

评估与准备：技师将评估您的身体状况，并根据需要调整治疗计划。您可以选择穿着舒适的衣物，以便治疗。

拨筋疗法：技师将使用专业技巧针对特定的肌肉和筋膜，在整个疗程中根据您的反馈实时调整压力。

治疗后评估：疗程结束后，技师会与您讨论效果，评估治疗结果，并提供后续建议。

后续护理：根据治疗效果，技师会建议后续疗程，以确保获得最佳效果。
